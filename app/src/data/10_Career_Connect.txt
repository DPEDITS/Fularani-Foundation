    About the Programme
    Career Connect is a structured career exposure and mentorship programme for students in Fularani Foundation's education network — designed to close the aspiration gap that separates talented rural students from knowledge of the careers available to them.

    Most students in rural Odisha know of three career paths: government job, teaching, or farming. Career Connect expands this horizon by bringing working professionals — doctors, engineers, entrepreneurs, civil servants, journalists, artists — directly to the students through in-person sessions, virtual talks, and immersive visits.
    You cannot aspire to what you cannot see. Career Connect makes sure that every student in our network can see — in vivid, human detail — a future worth working towards.
    Programme Activities
    
      🎤Speaker SessionsMonthly in-person or virtual talks by professionals from diverse fields — sharing their journeys, challenges, and career pathways.
      🏢Workplace VisitsOrganised visits to hospitals, factories, offices, courts, media houses and universities — giving students direct exposure.
      📝CV & Interview PrepPractical workshops on resume writing, job application processes, and interview skills for Class 10–12 students.
      🎓Higher Education GuidanceInformation on colleges, entrance exams, scholarships and application processes — demystifying higher education access.
      🤝Mentorship PairingHigh-potential students paired with volunteer mentors from relevant professions for 6-month guided mentorship.
      🏆Achievement RecognitionAnnual Career Connect awards recognising students who achieve significant milestones in their educational journey.
    
    Who Speaks
    Career Connect has hosted sessions featuring civil servants (IAS/IPS officers), doctors, engineers, entrepreneurs, social workers, journalists, lawyers, and artists from Odisha and beyond. The Foundation maintains a growing volunteer mentor network across professions. Corporate professionals and alumni of leading institutions are especially invited to participate as speakers.
    CSR Opportunity
    Sponsor a series of 12 Career Connect sessions for one academic year (₹1–1.5 lakh): covering speaker honoraria, travel, AV equipment, and student workbooks. Your company's professionals can be part of the speaker roster — creating a meaningful employee engagement and CSR activity simultaneously.

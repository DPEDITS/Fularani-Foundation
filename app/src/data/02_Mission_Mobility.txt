    About the Mission
    Mission Mobility addresses one of the most overlooked barriers to healthcare and education in rural Odisha — the inability to afford regular transport. Thalassemia patients require blood transfusions every 21 days for life. Dialysis patients attend hospital 3 times a week. For families in remote blocks, a single trip can cost more than a day's wage.

    Through a fleet of electric vehicles and rickshaws, Mission Mobility provides free, reliable, scheduled transport — connecting patients to DHH Bhadrak, sub-district hospitals, and railway stations; and connecting students to tuition centres and schools.
    Transport is not a luxury — it is the difference between a child receiving a life-saving transfusion and not. Mission Mobility removes that barrier permanently.
    Service Areas
    
      🏥Hospital ConnectivityScheduled daily runs between Bhadrak DHH, sub-district hospitals and remote block headquarters.
      🚉Station TransfersConnecting patients arriving by train at Bhadrak Railway Station to the District Hospital and back.
      🎒Student RunsMorning and evening runs for school and tuition centre students from underserved communities.
      ⚡Electric FleetZero-emission e-rickshaws and EVs — reducing operating cost and environmental impact simultaneously.
      📋Beneficiary RegistryRegistered patients and students on a priority list — ensuring consistent, predictable service.
      🤝Driver PartnershipPartnering with local EV operators under the Padhi Enterprises model for sustainable operations.
    
    Impact
    
      Free transport operational for thalassemia patients attending DHH Bhadrak
      Zero-fare rides for registered beneficiaries — no cash exchange required
      EV fleet reduces per-trip cost to near-zero compared to diesel alternatives
      Station-to-hospital transfer for outstation patients cuts access time by 60%+
      Student transport enables 50+ children to attend tuition centres daily
    
    
Transport Partner — Nandighosh Roadx Motors Pvt. Ltd.

  
    
      🤝 Nandighosh Roadx Motors Pvt. Ltd.
      Fularani Foundation has partnered with Nandighosh Roadx Motors Pvt. Ltd. to deliver Mission Mobility's free transport services across Bhadrak district. Nandighosh Roadx Motors, a trusted name in electric mobility solutions in Odisha, has joined hands with the Foundation to provide dedicated EV transport for thalassemia patients, dialysis patients and students — free of charge.
      This partnership brings together the Foundation's community outreach network and Nandighosh Roadx Motors' operational expertise in EV fleet management — creating a sustainable, zero-emission transport model that benefits the most vulnerable patients of Bhadrak district.

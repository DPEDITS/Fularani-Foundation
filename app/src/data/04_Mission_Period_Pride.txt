    About the Mission
    Mission Period Pride confronts one of India's most persistent public health and gender equity failures: menstrual health stigma and the lack of affordable, hygienic sanitary products for women and girls in rural and semi-urban Odisha. The programme combines awareness campaigns, school-based workshops, distribution drives, and transport support to create a holistic, dignity-centred intervention.

  
  
  
  


  
  

    Working in partnership with Padhi Enterprises for transport logistics and community-based women's organisations for outreach, Mission Period Pride reaches girls in schools, women in SHGs, and healthcare workers who lack basic menstrual hygiene knowledge or access.
    A girl who misses school every month due to lack of sanitary access loses 60+ school days a year. Period Pride is not just health — it is education, equity, and opportunity.
    Programme Pillars
    
      🎓School WorkshopsInteractive menstrual health sessions in government schools — breaking taboo and building factual awareness among adolescent girls and boys.
      🛍️Distribution DrivesFree and subsidised sanitary pad distribution to students, SHG members and women in underserved communities.
      👩‍⚕️ASHA & ANM TrainingCapacity building for frontline health workers to integrate menstrual health counselling into their routine visits.
      🚐Transport AccessPartner transport (Padhi Enterprises) enables the programme to reach remote blocks without dedicated Foundation vehicles.
      ♀️Gender Equity IntegrationAll sessions include a gender equity component — addressing discrimination, body autonomy, and women's rights.
      📢Community CampaignsVillage-level awareness campaigns using street plays, posters, and community radio to normalise menstruation.
    
    Impact
    
      Workshops delivered in multiple schools across Bhadrak district
      Thousands of sanitary products distributed in campaign drives
      Collaboration with women's self-help groups for sustained distribution
      Integration with Mission Education — combining academic and health support for the same beneficiaries
      Transport partnership model replicable to any district in Odisha

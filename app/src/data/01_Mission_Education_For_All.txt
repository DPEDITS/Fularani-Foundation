    About the Mission
    Mission Education for All is Fularani Foundation's founding programme — born from a single act of teaching one child that grew into a multi-centre model serving 400+ students today. The programme operates across 5+ schools and community centres in Bhadrak and surrounding areas, delivering free tuition, mentorship, and scholarships to first-generation learners.


  
  

    A unique chain model ensures sustainability: graduates return to teach the next cohort, multiplying impact without proportionally increasing cost. Today the programme has produced 12+ scholarship recipients who are pursuing higher education.
    "Education is not the filling of a pail, but the lighting of a fire." Every child in our programme is a flame that will light others.
    What We Deliver
    
      📚Free Tuition CentresDaily classes in core subjects — Maths, Science, English, Hindi — for Class 1 to 10 students across 5+ centres.
      🎓Scholarship Programme12+ merit and need-based scholarships awarded to high-potential students from low-income families for higher education.
      🔄Chain Teaching ModelGraduated students return as volunteer teachers — sustaining the programme and building community ownership.
      🏫School PartnershipsActive partnerships with 5+ government and community schools to supplement formal education with remedial support.
      🌱Foundational LiteracyBasic numeracy, Hindi and English literacy tutoring for out-of-school and over-age learners in communities.
      💼Career ConnectProfessional exposure sessions connecting rural students with working professionals, career pathways, and higher education options.
    
    Jeevan Kaushal — Life Skills
    Beyond academics, every student goes through the Jeevan Kaushal module — life skills training covering practical cooking and nutrition, personal finance fundamentals, self-reliance habits, and interpersonal communication. This ensures graduates are not just academically prepared but holistically capable of navigating adult life.

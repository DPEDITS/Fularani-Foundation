    About the Mission
    Mission Green is Fularani Foundation's flagship environmental programme — a large-scale, community-driven afforestation initiative spanning 30 districts and 314 blocks across Odisha. Each plantation site covers 3 to 100 acres, creating green corridors, restoring degraded land, and building community stewardship over natural resources.

  
  
    
    
  


  
  

    The programme operates in alignment with state and national environmental policies and is positioned to contribute to Odisha's climate commitments. Local youth, women's self-help groups, and school students are central to every plantation drive — making Mission Green as much a social programme as an environmental one.
    314 blocks. 30 districts. Every tree planted is a vote for the next generation's right to breathe clean air and drink clean water.
    Programme Components
    
      🌳Mass Plantation DrivesOrganised plantation events across 314 blocks with community participation, local species selection, and survival tracking.
      🗺️30-District CoverageActive plantation sites across all 30 districts of Odisha — creating a statewide green network.
      📐3 to 100 Acres Per SiteSite-appropriate scale — from roadside micro-plantation to large-scale degraded land restoration.
      👩‍🌾Community StewardshipLocal SHGs and youth groups trained as custodians — ensuring survival rates through post-plantation care.
      🏫School GreeningPlantation drives in school campuses integrating environmental education into the programme.
      📊Impact TrackingGeo-tagged plantation records, survival rate monitoring, and annual impact reports for donors and government.
    
    Waste to Wealth — Circular Economy
    Mission Green is complemented by the Waste to Wealth programme — a circular economy initiative developed in partnership with Gnufox Pvt. Ltd. and Bhadrak Municipality. The programme converts organic and dry waste into productive resources through composting, upcycling, and an e-Wallet incentive system that rewards households for responsible waste segregation.
    Krishi-Sakha AI
    The Foundation's environmental portfolio also includes Krishi-Sakha AI — an AI-powered crop diagnostics tool developed in collaboration with MITS (Devraj Mohapatra). Aligned with Odisha's AI Policy 2025, Krishi-Sakha helps farmers identify crop diseases, access advisory inputs, and reduce pesticide use — connecting environmental health with agricultural productivity.
    CSR Opportunity

A ₹5 lakh grant can plant and sustain 5,000+ trees in a named grove — a permanent, visible legacy in your company's name.

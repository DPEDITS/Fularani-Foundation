    About the Mission
    The Donation Wall is a beautifully simple idea with profound impact: a publicly accessible shelf installed at New District Hospital, Bhadrak, stocked with essential non-medical items that hospitalised patients and their families often desperately need — food packets, toiletries, medicines, clothes, and comfort items.


  
  

    Donations are accepted from the public, corporates, and community groups. The wall is managed by Foundation volunteers in coordination with hospital staff, ensuring items reach those in genuine need with dignity and no bureaucratic barrier.
    A patient in hospital has already lost everything — income, routine, comfort. The Donation Wall gives something back: the knowledge that the community cares.
    What the Wall Provides
    
      🍱Food & NutritionPackaged food, biscuits, nutritional supplements and dry rations for patients and their accompanying family members.
      🧴Personal HygieneSoap, toothbrush, toothpaste, comb, sanitary pads — basic hygiene items often unavailable to patients from remote areas.
      👕Clothing & LinenClean clothes, blankets and linen for patients admitted in emergency without preparation.
      💊OTC MedicinesBasic over-the-counter medicines: paracetamol, antacids, ORS, bandages — vetted and stocked with hospital coordination.
      📖Children's ItemsBooks, toys, drawing materials for children admitted to the paediatric ward or accompanying parents.
      ♻️Community DonationsOpen to public donations — individuals, schools, offices, and clubs can restock the wall as a seva act.
    
    How It Works
    
      The wall is a physical installation at New District Hospital, Bhadrak — visible, accessible and permanently stocked
      Foundation volunteers conduct bi-weekly restocking and inventory management
      Hospital social workers identify and guide high-need patients to the wall
      Corporate and individual donors can sponsor specific item categories or full monthly restocking
      Impact is photographed and shared with donors monthly

    About the Mission
    Healthy Bhadrak Open Gym is a first-of-its-kind community fitness initiative in Bhadrak district — establishing a free, open-access outdoor gym at the campus of DHH (District Headquarters Hospital) Bhadrak. The facility is designed to serve patients in recovery, caregivers under stress, hospital staff, and the general public — integrating physical wellness into the hospital environment.

    With official support from the district health administration, the Open Gym provides cardio equipment, resistance training stations, and a dedicated yoga and rehabilitation space — all free of charge, with no membership or fees.
    Health does not begin and end at the prescription pad. A community that moves together heals together. The Open Gym brings preventive health to the doorstep of those who need it most.

    Why a Hospital Campus?
    Placing the gym at DHH Bhadrak is strategic: patients with chronic conditions like thalassemia, diabetes, and hypertension — who visit the hospital regularly — are the population most in need of supervised physical activity. Caregivers under physical and emotional strain also benefit directly. The hospital campus ensures the facility reaches its highest-need users naturally, without requiring outreach or transportation.

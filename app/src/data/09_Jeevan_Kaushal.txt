    About the Programme
    Jeevan Kaushal (Life Skills) is a structured, practical training programme for youth and adolescents in Fularani Foundation's education network. While Mission Education for All focuses on academic learning, Jeevan Kaushal fills the gap that formal schooling systematically ignores: the skills needed to navigate adult life with confidence, health, and financial stability.

  
  

    The programme is delivered through workshops, peer sessions, and hands-on practice at Foundation centres and partner schools — covering four core domains: cooking and nutrition, personal finance, self-reliance habits, and interpersonal communication.
    A student who can solve quadratic equations but cannot cook a meal, manage a bank account, or communicate with confidence is not truly educated. Jeevan Kaushal closes that gap.
    Curriculum Modules
    
      🍳Cooking & NutritionPractical cooking skills, food hygiene, balanced diet planning, and understanding of nutrition — for boys and girls equally.
      💰Personal FinanceSavings habits, banking basics, UPI and digital payments, household budgeting, and understanding interest and loans.
      🧠Self-RelianceProblem-solving skills, emotional regulation, goal-setting, time management, and building daily positive habits.
      🗣️CommunicationConfident spoken Hindi and English, letter writing, public speaking, interview preparation, and professional etiquette.
      🏡Household ManagementFirst aid basics, home safety, water and electricity conservation, and understanding household documentation.
      🤝Civic AwarenessRights and responsibilities, government scheme awareness, community participation, and democratic values.
    
    Delivery Model
    
      Integrated into Foundation education centres as Saturday modules
      Delivered by trained youth volunteers and community resource persons
      Hands-on and activity-based — no rote learning, only practice
      Separate cohorts for boys and girls where culturally appropriate
      Certificate of participation issued by the Foundation on completion
